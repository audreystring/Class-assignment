Meet the Photographer

Audrey String is an American cinematographer and photographer known for her work in film and photography. Audrey String's business includes portraits, headshots, business advertising, and more. Audrey String has worked with MLS Lawns and Landscape business to promote their work through photography and film in 2022.

Audrey String's recent work includes being a Video Manager for the University of Cincinnati Football Program, which involves filming and editing both everyday practices and games. Also taking part in a hands on experience with live sports events for ESPN+. Learning the ins and outs of a live sports production, while also operating cameras from different angles and shading cameras within the control booth.
